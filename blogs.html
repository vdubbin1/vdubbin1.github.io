What is it?

Unlike regular crime, Cyber Crime is often stealth and affects both individuals and businesses alike. As a general rule, cyber crime is committed for either financial gain or with intent to cause disruption to a business, group or network.

Types of Cyber Crime include:

  • Ransomware attacks
  • password decryption
  • BEC (Business Email Compromise)
  • Hacking - including DDoS(distributed denial-of-service) attacks.
  • malware attacks - including viruses, worms, trojans, spyware.
  • cyber fraud - including phishing, spear phishing, fishing and whaling.
